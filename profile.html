ABOUT EGSPJ INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

The EGSPJ International School is our new International school launched by the prestigious G. S. Pillay & Sons Educational & Charitable Trust, Nagapattinam.

The EGSPJ International School is developed from Pre-school to Class Eight. The school follows the curriculum designed by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).However, within that curricular framework; the school will aim for more.

Initially, in the year 2020-2021, the school starts with classes from Pre-school to Standard VIII. Additional classes will be added in the forthcoming years .Now the school is recognized by CBSE up to STD X. The school has formed a caring and nurturing team of faculty and the supporting staff who are very passionate about their job. The location is within the city limit but away from the congestion and pollution with a lush green campus. The architecture of the school provides for the ideal environment for exciting learning.

Our Montessori follows International Standard Montessori curriculum which caters Children aged between 2 ½ and 5½ , The EGSPJ International School - Montessori is going to be one of the finest and Pure Montessori way of school in Nagapattinam District.

The School Provides spacious class rooms and limited number of children in each class room (i.e., Maximum 28 Children) to give quality education

Here in The G. S. Our Motto

Cultivating excellence in every student.

Chairman Message

Our programmes, approaches, and outcomes have evolved over years to create an exceptional work environment where our faculty can teach, learn, and grow as valued individuals. We strongly believe that highly qualified teachers provide the stimulus for academic achievement in all disciplines. The diversity and qualifications of our faculty and the innate curiosity of our students have contributed to the continued success of EGSPJ International School.

We are more focused on the original vision with which the institution was founded. We have adopted internationalism as a practiced culture to encourage youth to develop as confident global citizens and make worthy contributions to improve the world. It will be our constant endeavor to tailor the learning experience to each individual cohort as the course progresses

principal message

EIS is an international learning community with English as the language of instruction. In keeping with our commitment to diversity, we offer students in grades 1-12 a comprehensive program for English language learners (ELL).

We offer a rigorous curriculum based on ICSE and CBSE instuction practices. Our goal is to prepare students for their future — the next grade, school, college or college and the challenges of the 21st century.

The school provides a safe and spacious learning environment where students have the opportunity to develop their love of learning and enjoy challenges. At EIS, values such as mutual respect, personal responsibility, equity, and compassion are cultivated. We value the development of students’ creativity, critical thinking, and collaborative skills. We believe that every student learns differently, and we strive to accommodate the wide range of learning styles that pass through our doors and fill our classrooms each day.

At EIS, we promote a healthy and balanced lifestyle with a broad academic program and a wide range of extracurricular opportunities through our sports, arts and recreation programs. Likewise, we believe that students learn through their own actions and that community engagement provides opportunities to make unique learning connections and support local civic endeavors.
