INSURANCE / WITHDRAWALS / LEGAL DISPUTES

Insurance

At EGSPJ all health and safety measures including group/personal supervision are embedded in the school’s systems. However, inspite of the best care and facilities at school or at home unforeseen eventualities might occur. In order to cover such eventualities, the school takes out a Group Insurance policy covering the students and their parents every year.

Withdrawals

Students wishing to withdraw should give at least one term’s notice. Such notice should be given in writing and addressed to the principal. In case of withdrawal, fee pertaining to the term falling in the notice period shall be payable by the student.



Legal Disputes

Legal disputes, if any, shall be settled only in the courts having their jurisdiction in NAGAPATTINAM district.
